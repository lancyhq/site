Good to Know

Common
questions.

Why no WordPress?

Custom static builds are faster, more secure, cheaper to run, and fully portable. You own clean HTML and CSS that any developer can maintain — not a platform you're locked into.

What does hosting cost?

Static sites on Cloudflare Pages are free — unlimited bandwidth, global CDN, automatic SSL. Domain registration runs ~$10–$15/year. That's typically your only recurring cost.

How does payment work?

50% deposit before work starts, 50% on delivery. Bank transfer, PayPal, Venmo Business, or check. Card payments add a 3% processing fee. All quotes are fixed-price — no surprises.

What do I need to provide?

Your logo, written content, and any photos. We handle everything else. If you need copywriting or photography sourcing, we offer those as add-ons.

How long does a project take?

Launchpad: 2–3 weeks. Presence: 3–5 weeks. Authority: 5–7 weeks. Timelines depend heavily on how quickly you can provide content and feedback.

What if I need something bigger?

The Enterprise tier handles complex builds — large sites, custom backends, integrations, client portals. Contact us and we'll scope it out properly.
